Free registration

The workshop

The workshop on computational thinking in education, to be held 23 June 2016 at RCC Harvard, will pay a tribute to the visionaries who 50 years ago started to transform the way computer science is used in education. Top educators and researchers from different backgrounds will share what they are doing now, 50 years later. And everyone who attends will be invited to share their views on the use of computer science and computational thinking in education in the future... perhaps in another 50 years :-)
